CDOE

The distance can never be an obstacle to learning. Explore and enhance your knowledge through our Online and Distance Learning!


Karunya Institute of Technology and Science was established in 1986 with a vision to find solutions to human problems and has a significant mark in education, knowledge sharing and teaching. It has now stepped into a new realm of teaching and learning through the Online and Distance Learning mode to reach the unreached, marginalized and less privileged learners.

Online Learning

KITS is giving open access to its self-learning materials to all its students by making it available on the Website. The material is prepared by faculty who are experts in those concerned courses.

Student Orientation

orientation session to the newly admitted Online Education students to make them aware of the academic structure, processes, features, career counselling and other details related to their programmes.

Affordable Fee

Affordability should not be a barrier to learning. The fees of all the courses are kept minimum to help candidates with a professional aspiration to help them in pursuing their dream jobs.


E- PORTAL

KITS Eduserve is an extraordinary and easy to use online platform for all the academic activities, it provides ALL THE TIME fast and reliable online connectivity that keeps its students to be always connected with the university.

Competent Faculty

KITS has world-class faculty members with strong research and teaching experience. All the teachers involved in ODL and OL modes are doctorates with minimum 2 decades of teaching experience.

Student support

There are a wide range of support services available to ensure that all students fulfil their potential, achieve their goals and enjoy their time at KITS
