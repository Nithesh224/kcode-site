Overview

With a vision to develop influential leaders to find solutions to societal problems, The School of Management strives to transform students, both in their personal and professional front to take on the corporate responsibility in the global context without losing their foot locally. This is made possible by Industry relevant curriculum and competent faculty members who come with a blend of Industry and Academic experiences.

Schoool of Mangement Offers MBA Specialisation in Finance | Marketing | HR | Logistics and Supply Chain Management.

The School of Management, has achieved the unique distinction of being the first School of Management to get the Accreditation from Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (UK) at Post Graduate Program level (MBA) in the State of Tamil Nadu. It, also has made a mark in the academic arena with an excellent placement record, offering career opportunities with high salary package.

Facilities

The state-of-the-art facilities, include 24x7 Wi-Fi connectivity, high-end computers installed with software’s like Statistical Package for Social Sciences (SPSS), Tally ERP 9.1 and Prowess IQ from Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy (CMIE). The School of Management has a well-stocked library with around 6500 books on a variety of titles and volumes on Business and Management, that provide academic support to students, who access our Library digitally. The University, also subscribes to various International E- Journals, like EBSCO-host, Emerald etc.,

The School of Management collaborates with ACCA (United Kingdom), a century old Global Professional Accounting body to equip the students with Professional skills in Accounting, Finance and Strategy, which ultimately make our students one of the most sought-after finance professionals in the world. National Stock Exchange also has entered into a strategic alliance to train our students in the Capital Market front.

In the last two and a half decades, the School of Management has groomed 3000+ illustrious managers who are occupying strategic positions in Indian and overseas MNC’s like Airtel, Amazon, Asian Paints, Axis Bank, Berger Paints, HDB Financial Services, BNP Paribas, Colgate Palmolive, Decathlon, Grant Thornton, HFFC, ICICI Securities, IDBI Federal, NEEYAMO, IndusInd Bank, Jaro Education, Naukri.Com, MaxVal, MRHM, New Age Sys Solutions, PHOTON, KPMG, TTK, Wildcraft, ZOHO, Maersk, Dachser India P. Lts, TVS Logistics, Ashok Leyland warehousing, Joveen Logistics (I) P Ltd, Smart Shipping and Logistics and much more. Apart from this, our graduates have been to highly ranked Institutions such as University of West of England, (UK), University of Canberra, Australia to continue their MS degree in International Management.

The School of Management has over 100+ publications, both in National and International Journals which are indexed in Scopus and Web of Sciences. Our faculty also work on Government Funded projects, and develop case studies exclusively for classroom discussions. Select faculty members have to their credit books published from renowned publishers, like Pearson, & McGraw Hill.

The faculty and students do research in frontier areas like digital marketing, consumer behavior, cost management practices, stock market volatility, logistics and third-party issues, work life balance, emotional intelligence etc., Our faculty members have recently embarked on a research journey in, block chain technology applications in health, and agricultural sectors and also pitched in green mobility solutions for public transport system in a bigger way. Thus, fulfilling the Industry 4.0 recommendations.
